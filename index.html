The Venue

The Homestead is a classic New England 18th century working farm in Lyman, Maine. Easily reached from I-95, the Homestead is conveniently located a half hour south of the Portland International Jetport, an hour and a half north of Boston Logan International Airport, an hour and twenty minutes north of Manchester Regional Airport, and minutes from the resort towns of Kennebunk and Kennebunkport.

The Homestead Farm
74 Old North Berwick Rd, Lyman, ME 04002
